**March 25, 2026**  PicoClaw Team announces WeChat support is now available

Your AI assistant can now chat with you directly inside WeChat

---

## WeChat + PicoClaw = ?

From Discord to QQ, from DingTalk to Feishu, PicoClaw already integrates with 18 chat platforms

But the question we hear most from users in China has always been:

"When is WeChat support coming?"

Today, that question has an answer

PicoClaw now officially supports personal WeChat account integration!

No enterprise WeChat, no Official Account, no Service Account needed —

Just use your own WeChat account, scan a QR code, and PicoClaw becomes your WeChat AI assistant

All conversations are processed locally — no third-party servers involved

---

## Done in Two Steps

The whole process takes less than a minute.

Click the "WeChat" channel in the web UI

![Web UI](../../../assets/blog/wechat-support/webui.png)

Or run one command in the terminal:

```
picoclaw auth weixin
```

![Terminal](../../../assets/blog/wechat-support/cli.png)

Scan the QR code and your WeChat becomes an AI assistant portal

If prompted to update, follow the on-screen instructions

**That's it!**

![WeChat chat](../../../assets/blog/wechat-support/chat.jpg)

Say hello to ClawBot — PicoClaw will reply automatically

---

## What Can It Do?

The experience is identical to every other platform:

- Everyday chat, Q&A, translation
- Web search for real-time information
- File processing, code generation
- Reminders and scheduling
- Extend with any capability via MCP — query live stock prices, fetch the latest technical docs, or connect your own tools and services

Everything you can do on QQ, you can do on WeChat

---

## Things to Know

- This feature is rolling out gradually. If it doesn't work for you yet, join the community group or wait a short while
- The WeChat ClawBot plugin is an officially provided capability by Tencent. PicoClaw connects through the official API
- For security, we recommend running PicoClaw on your own device
- This feature currently supports personal use only — sharing with others or use in group chats is not yet supported
